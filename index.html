Tyler Chang
PhD Student at UC San Diego. 
Natural Language Processing / Cognitive Linguistics / Computational Linguistics.

I am a cognitive science PhD student at UCSD, affiliated with the Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute. I am interested in how people understand language, and how we can make NLP systems more human-like. I am currently interning at Amazon Alexa.


I completed my undergrad at Carleton College, majoring in math and cognitive science. During undergrad, I studied linguistics and mathematics in Japan and Hungary. In the past, I have worked on the Geo Machine Perception, Chrome, and Cloud AI Translation teams at Google.

Feel free to contact me at tachang@ucsd.edu!
